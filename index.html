LigAfric Technology Ltd

Vision

To become Africa's leading digital ecosystem that connects people, businesses, professionals, and communities, creating opportunities for economic growth, collaboration, innovation, and sustainable development.

Mission

To provide an inclusive digital platform that empowers farmers, businesses, professionals, sports organizations, and community groups by enabling them to connect, trade, collaborate, learn, and achieve their economic potential.

Core Values

  • Innovation – Using technology to solve everyday challenges.
  • Integrity – Building trust through transparency and accountability.
  • Inclusivity – Serving rural and urban communities alike.
  • Collaboration – Connecting people and organizations for mutual growth.
  • Empowerment – Creating opportunities for individuals and businesses.
  • Excellence – Delivering reliable, high-quality digital services.
  • Sustainability – Supporting long-term economic and social development.
