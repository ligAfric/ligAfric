LigAfric

Building the interconnected digital super-app ecosystem for Africa's future. Connecting commerce, community, and talent on a single platform.

Our Identity

👁️ Vision

To become Africa's leading digital ecosystem that connects people, businesses, professionals, and communities, creating opportunities for economic growth, collaboration, innovation, and sustainable development.

🎯 Mission

To provide an inclusive digital platform that empowers farmers, businesses, professionals, sports organizations, and community groups by enabling them to connect, trade, collaborate, learn, and access opportunities regardless of their location.

💎 Core Values
  • Innovation – Using technology to solve everyday challenges.
  • Integrity – Building trust through transparency and accountability.
  • Inclusivity – Serving rural and urban communities alike.
  • Collaboration – Connecting people and organizations for mutual growth.
  • Empowerment – Creating opportunities for individuals and businesses.
  • Excellence – Delivering reliable, high-quality digital services.
  • Sustainability – Supporting long-term economic and social development.

The Core Ecosystem Pillars

🌾 Smart Agriculture & Business

Connecting smallholder farmers, cooperatives, and local businesses directly to regional marketplaces, logistics providers, and supply chains.

⚽ Sports & Communities

Empowering local athletes, academies, and professional service networks with digital management profiles and talent discovery tools.

Ecosystem Implementation Roadmap

Phase 1: Foundation (Minimum Viable Product)

Goal: Build users and prove the platform's value.

Launching the essential platform features:

  • User registration and profiles
  • Secure login
  • Marketplace for farmers
  • Marketplace for businesses
  • Messaging between users
  • Search and filters
  • Ratings and reviews
  • Admin dashboard
Phase 2: Scale & Ecosystem Integration

Expanding into full cross-border service matching, integrated sports scouting networks, and unified ecosystem analytics.
